# 📜 Code of Conduct

## 🤝 Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

---

## 🌟 Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and inclusive
* Communicate professionally
* Help others when possible
* Accept feedback gracefully

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment or discrimination
* Spam or irrelevant contributions
* Offensive language or personal attacks
* Any form of misconduct

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement

Project maintainers (PKs) will review and take action against any unacceptable behavior.

---

## 📢 Reporting Issues

If you experience or notice any inappropriate behavior, report it to the maintainers.

---

## 💙 Note

This project follows standard open-source community guidelines to ensure a positive experience for everyone.
